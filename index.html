About me

Hi! I'm Michal, an ultimate year student of Computer Engineering. I am currently based in London, UK, and originally from Bratislava Slovakia. I still split my time between these two cities, so you may bump into me in either.

My academic and professional interests focus on computer architecture, high performance computing and digital design. I thoroughly enjoy digging deep down into the details of the problems at hand and finding insights, building from the bottom up.

During my free time you might catch me trying to get an electric fomula car running in the pit garage of Imperial College, cleaning and repairing old mechanical watch mechanisms under a microscope or learning about an assortment of science and engineering topics.
